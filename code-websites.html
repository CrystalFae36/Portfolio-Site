BPA Competition Site 2024: Stage Fright

Github Repository

This is the first real site I ever made. It was made alongside two other people on my team: one designer and one developer. I did much of the style work and structure of many pages on the site. This was a submission to Business Professionals of America, and was coded following their prompt, which was to make a website based on a small rock band called "Stage Fright." The other developer handled pretty much all the scripting, but (almost) all the style was me. We did start with a template, which I'll link here, but as you can see, we changed a lot of it and really made it our own. Honestly, really proud of how this one turned out. I did all the writing for the site, made the About page, the News page and all subsequent article pages, the Home page, the Contact page, and the Discography page, as well as all subsequent album pages with their songs on them. All of this was done in around six months or so, maybe a little bit less.
